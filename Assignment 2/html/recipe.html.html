Mamas Homemade Lemon Meringue Pie

One 9-ich pie

Prep and Cook Time:

  • 30 Min-Prep time
  • 10 Min-Cook time
  • 40 Min-Total time
  • 8 Servings

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons all purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons cornstach
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 11/2 cups water
  • 2 lemons, juiced and zested
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 4 egg yolks, beaten
  • 1(9 inch pie crust, baked)
  • 4 egg whites
  • 6 tablespoons white sugar

Directions:

  1. Preheat Oven:

    Preheat oven to 350 degrees F(175 degrees C).

  2. Make Lemon Filling:

    In a medium saucepan...
    • Whisk toether 1 cup sugar, flour, cornstach, and salt.
    • Cook over medium-high heat, string frequently, until mixture comes to a boil.
    • Stir in butter.
    • Place egg yolks in a small bowl and gradually whisk.
    • Whisk egg yolk mixture back into remaining sugar mixture.
    • Bring to a boil and continue to cook while string constantly until thick.
    • Remove from heat.
    • Pour filling into baked pastry shell.

  3. Make Meringue:

    In a large glass or metal bowl...
    • Whip egg whites until foamy.
    • Add sugar gradually, and continue to whip until stiff peaks form.
    • Spread meringue over pie, sealing the endes at the crust.

  4. Bake:

    Bake in preheated oven for 10 minutes, or until meringue is golden brown.
