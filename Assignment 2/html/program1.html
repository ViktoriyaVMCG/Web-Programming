Today's special story: Everything You Need to Know About Organic Foods

The first thing to keep in mind is that the term "organic" can be applied to a variety of different kinds of foods. The term can be used on agricultural products, and on meat, poultry, eggs and dairy products. And it also applies to the methods used to process organically grown foods in preparing them for market or to retard spoilage.

Organically Grown Crops, Meat, Poultry, and Dairy:

  • Organic products are grown:
    • Without the use of GMOs
    • Without the use of synthetic pesticides
    • Without the use of growth regulators
    • Non-hazardous to human health
  • Herbal Organic must be grown:
    • From eco-friendly seeds and seedlings
    • Using only organic bio-fertilizers to fertilize the soil and biological products to protect plants from diseases and pests.
  • Livestock and poultry:
    • Must be produced from livestock and ecologically grown poultry
    • Must be fed with eco-friendly feeds without using growth hormones, antibiotics, and various feed additives.
    • Conditions of detention must ensure freedom of movement and exercise.
    • Veterinary services should monitor the health of livestock and poultry.
  • Milk and dairy products must be obtained from ecologically grown animals.
  • Organic food can be identified by "organic" labeling on food."Organic" label means that the product is certified and complies with the requirements of production and processing following the rules established by the USDA.
  • Organic production helps to protect the soil and groundwater creates favorable conditions for the life of animals and plants, and also contributes to the promotion of human health.

REMEMBER: The taste of products grown in environmental conditions is expressed brighter and tastier!
